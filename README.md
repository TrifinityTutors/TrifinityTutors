# 👋 Welcome to Trifinity Tutors

## 🎓 Find the Right Tutor, Near You

Trifinity Tutors is a platform designed to connect students with qualified tutors in their local area. Whether you're preparing for school exams, competitive exams, college subjects, or learning a new skill, our goal is to make finding the right tutor simple and accessible.

### 🚀 What We Do
- 🔍 Search for tutors nearby
- 📚 Find subject-specific experts
- ⭐ Compare tutor profiles and ratings
- 📍 Location-based tutor discovery
- 🤝 Connect students and educators

### 🎯 Our Mission
To bridge the gap between students and quality education by making tutor discovery fast, reliable, and location-aware.

### 🛠️ Current Development
- Tutor search and filtering
- Location-based recommendations
- Student and tutor profiles
- Booking and communication features
- Review and rating system

### 🌱 Vision
We believe every student deserves access to quality education. Trifinity Tutors aims to become the go-to platform for personalized learning and tutor discovery.
